Governance Engine

Constitutional Governance Execution Architecture

⸻

Executive Summary

The Governance Engine is the central execution mechanism of Agility Game Governance.

If the Governance Principles define:

What Governance Must Never Violate

then the Governance Engine defines:

How Governance Operates

The Governance Engine transforms:

Intent

into:

Trust

through:

* standards
* validation
* evidence
* scoring
* assurance
* promotion

The Governance Engine continuously converts uncertainty into confidence.

⸻

Purpose

The purpose of the Governance Engine is to answer:

Are We In Control?

for every governable object.

Examples:

* Worlds
* Missions
* Agents
* Departments
* Products
* Services
* Architectures
* Organizations

The Governance Engine is therefore the operational heart of governance.

⸻

Governance Philosophy

Traditional governance often follows:
```
Create
      ↓
Review
      ↓
Approve
      ↓
Operate
```
This model creates bottlenecks.

The Governance Engine follows:
```
Create
      ↓
Validate
      ↓
Evidence
      ↓
Assure
      ↓
Promote
```
continuously.

Governance becomes an active capability rather than a periodic activity.

⸻

Constitutional Responsibilities

The Governance Engine is responsible for:

* governance execution
* validation orchestration
* evidence collection
* confidence generation
* promotion decisions
* governance telemetry

The Governance Engine is not responsible for:

* defining truth
* defining constitutional principles
* assigning accountability

Those responsibilities belong elsewhere within the Foundations layer.

Governance Lifecycle

Every governance object follows the same lifecycle.
```
Created
      ↓
Explored
      ↓
Validated
      ↓
Operational
      ↓
Certified
      ↓
Retired
```
⸻

Created

Object exists.

Trust does not.

⸻

Explored

Learning occurs.

Governance is intentionally lightweight.

⸻

Validated

Evidence begins accumulating.

Repeatability becomes measurable.

⸻

Operational

The object is depended upon.

Continuous governance applies.

⸻

Certified

Trust has become sustained.

Assurance is formally recognized.

⸻

Retired

Object leaves active governance.

Historical evidence remains available.

Validators

Does Reality Match Expectation?

Outputs

FAIL
INCONCLUSIVE
